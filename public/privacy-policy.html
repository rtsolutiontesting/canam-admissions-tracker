Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

CANAM IAPPLY Data Extractor ("we", "our", "us") is a legitimate data management tool designed for education consultants and admissions teams. This privacy policy explains how we handle data in our application.

2. Data Collection

We only process data that you explicitly provide through Google Sheets integration. We do not collect personal information without your consent. All data processing occurs within your own Google Sheets and Firebase account.

3. Data Usage

Data is used solely for the purpose of tracking and managing university admissions information. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purposes.

4. Data Security

All data is stored securely in Firebase with authentication required for access. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

This application uses:

  • Google Sheets API - for reading and updating your spreadsheets
  • Firebase - for secure data storage and authentication
  • Google AI Studio - for data extraction from web pages
